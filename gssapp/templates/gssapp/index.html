TARGET & TEACH

Target n Teach (TnT) is a program started by the company to develop the quality of graduates. Apart from the formal education, we believe skill development is one of the most important factors in deciding an individual's ,and consequently, country's future. Internationally, skill development is given more importance than just formal education. In this cyber age, where people spend more time with computers and mobile than people, technical skills are the must. Formal education is not up to date, thus we need to switch to these unconventional methods to keep us equipped according to the latest and ever changing technology. We here aim to encourage all people (not only engineers) to engage with technology along with books , to make a significant contribution and do something revolutionary.
