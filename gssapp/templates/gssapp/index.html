TARGET n TEACH

Aim

Target n Teach (TnT) is a programme started by comapny to develop the quality of graduates. Apart from formal education we believe skill development is one of the most important factor in deciding an individual's ,and consequently, country's future. Internationally, skill development is given more importance than just formal education. It's for people to equip themselves in this competitive worldand also who want to work in a field different from their major. Institutions like Harvard and several comapnies concentrate on developing technical skills of people to make them more efficient and productive. In this cyber age, where people spend more time with computers and mobile than people, technical skills are must. Formal education is not up to date, thus we need to switch to these unconventional methos to keep us equipped according to the latest and ever changing technology. In other countries, high school kids are making big bucks by developing apps and website and here a b.tech graduate struggles with a C/C++ code. We here aim to encourage all people (not only engineers) to engage with technology along with books , to make a significant contribution and do something revolutionary.

Vision

It's an inspiration from the recent programmes by government like Digitalising India and strong focus on Skill Development. There is a huge number of graduates (specially engineers), but still the quality is questionanle. Most of the companies have to run a training session after hiring them, for months, which is like a full formal education course, all because of lack in skills. This is raises a huge doubt on the education system. So, we started this programme to make up for this reason to make our graduates lot, the best in the world.

Future Plans

We will be starting with some mainstream workshops like Web Development, Android App Development, Pyhton, etc.., but soon we will be introducing more different topics like Photography,Different Audio and Video Softwares, Photoshop, etc.

Think Tank reffers to the group of developers this company will connect virtually. They together can share ideas, and work together on projects. Company will help them with this and encourage them. It"ll provide them resources and help them from this pool of people only. We strongly believe that we have an amazing quality of developers which can come up with some great ideas.
When soo many minds work together, we believe that miracles can happen. We will be hiring people from here only for our comapny's project. So it's like we will train them, then they will be automatically added in our think tank. And, they only will be our workforce in developing some great apps and websites.
